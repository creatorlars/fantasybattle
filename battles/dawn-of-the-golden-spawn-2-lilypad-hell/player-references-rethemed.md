# Dawn of the Golden Spawn 2: Lilypad Hell — Player References (Rethemed)

A scenario for **Warhammer Fantasy Battle 3rd Edition / Rogue Trader**
Written by Lars Larsen for 3D Roleplay Con VI, May 2nd–3rd 2026

> *"I've seen things these damn-phibians wouldn't believe... Attack ships on fire off the shoulder of Dendrax V... I watched the Golden Spawn glitter in the dark near the Resplendent Frond. All those moments will be lost in time, like eggs in the pan. Time to fry."*
> — last words of Imperial Guard Culinary Specialist **Jed Tannisport**, MIA in Lilypad Hell

Six factions of amphibians from across the stars converge on the Slann homeworld of **Lustriax Prime** to settle the invasion of its most sacred and dangerous wetland: **Lilypad Hell**.

---

## Player Reference #1 — Pad Master Kozmox & the Cuiteca Spawn

**Constellation:** Jaguar

### Bio
Kozmox has held the rank of Pad Master aboard Cuiteca for a decade, like his father. Refusing the augmented Bull Slann bodyguards owed his station, he marches with cousins and clutch-siblings from his own spawning-year, every one of whom holds a sinecure in the court of his ancestral orbital, Cuiteca ("Gold Pad").

### Initial Objective
Celebrate the hatching of every clutch.

### Relationships
| Character | Kozmox's view |
|---|---|
| Captain Krookomet | A tin-toad, blind to all finery. |
| Tepitronix the Xeno Charmer | His pink-skin show kept my court laughing for cycles. |
| Xochiton the Star Seeker | Perhaps this old psyker's gifts can serve my ends. |
| Sentinel Ninjorbit | Flattery will keep this venom tribe under my thumb. |
| Astro Priests Kukulbufon & Bubulkufax | These mud-caked bog-priests disgrace the Imperium. |

## Player Reference #2 — Tepitronix the Xeno Charmer

**Constellation:** Eagle

### Bio
Tepitronix "Tepi" and his Troglodyte companion Biglips have toured for many cycles, hauling their carnival of lobotomized humans they call "pinkies." Tepi has perfected the cortex-tap surgery that turns feral humans into compliant servitors. Biglips lives only for his next meal of a disobedient pinky.

### Initial Objective
Ink new performance contracts and evade the law.

### Relationships
| Character | Tepitronix's view |
|---|---|
| Pad Master Kozmox | The coin flows when we perform for the court at Cuiteca. |
| Captain Krookomet | He has writs out for the arrest of our entire carnival. |
| Xochiton the Star Seeker | An important client; pays in dangerous secrets. |
| Sentinel Ninjorbit | An advocate for uncollared pinkies; best avoided. |
| Astro Priests Kukulbufon & Bubulkufax | Useful guides who show us where wild pinkies hide. |

## Player Reference #3 — Captain Krookomet & the Bull Slann

**Constellation:** Alligator

### Bio
Captain Krookomet is a recently promoted officer who treats his oath of service to Star-Father Mazdamundi with utmost sincerity. He deploys only with Bull Slann decanted on the same day, from the same vat-pool. He despises aristocratic preening and is highly devout, dreaming one day of ascension to the rank of Alligator Warrior.

### Initial Objective
Prevent mayhem and maintain order.

### Relationships
| Character | Krookomet's view |
|---|---|
| Pad Master Kozmox | Evidence of the Throne's crumbling aristocracy. |
| Tepitronix the Xeno Charmer | A wanted felon dangerous to the Imperium. |
| Xochiton the Star Seeker | Another self-important envoy, no doubt. |
| Sentinel Ninjorbit | Star-Father's traitors, yet warriors deserving respect. |
| Astro Priests Kukulbufon & Bubulkufax | Seers of the ancient wisdom of the Alligator Warriors. |

## Player Reference #4 — Xochiton the Star Seeker & Pimblar Ono

**Constellation:** Jaguar

### Bio
Xochiton the Star Seeker has wandered the Frond Sector in his quest to decipher the mysteries of the Lost Data-Scrolls. He is hundreds of years old, and wields psyker power beyond the reckoning of most. He is here to witness whether the Dawn of the Golden Spawn dawns this time, accompanied by his apprentice Pimblar Ono.

### Initial Objective
Witness all prophecies come to pass.

### Relationships
| Character | Xochiton's view |
|---|---|
| Pad Master Kozmox | The Spawnfather must fulfill his destiny as egg protector. |
| Tepitronix the Xeno Charmer | A fine pinky-monger who supplied my litter bearers. |
| Captain Krookomet | A pawn on the star-board, a servant who must obey. |
| Sentinel Ninjorbit | Their long watch nears its prophesied end, again. |
| Astro Priests Kukulbufon & Bubulkufax | Bog-shamans, ignorant of the true Data-Scrolls. |

## Player Reference #5 — Sentinel Ninjorbit & the Lilypad Guardians

**Constellation:** Eagle

### Bio
Ninjorbit is the latest leader in a line of sentinels extending to the beginning of time, all of whom guarded the sacred underground spawning vats of the Slann nobility. Refusing the Star-Father, his glyph-painted tribes claim sole ownership of the vats. To Ninjorbit, this hatching is like any other from the past few hundred years.

### Initial Objective
Permit no desecration of this sacred site.

### Relationships
| Character | Ninjorbit's view |
|---|---|
| Pad Master Kozmox | An impressive Spawnfather who quickened all of his eggs. |
| Tepitronix the Xeno Charmer | A defiler who dares bring pink-skins to a sacred place. |
| Xochiton the Star Seeker | One who knows the secret ways, one who is wise. |
| Captain Krookomet | Imperial scum, unnecessary and intrusive. |
| Astro Priests Kukulbufon & Bubulkufax | Curiosities, primal beasts, and unwelcome guests. |

## Player Reference #6 — Astro Priests Kukulbufon & Bubulkufax

**Constellation:** Alligator

### Bio
Kukulbufon & Bubulkufax are from the deep frond-bogs, shaman leaders of small villages hidden from the great Slann Imperium. Signs unseen for six centuries convinced them to make this voyage, which they believe to be the fulfillment of a great prophecy told throughout time, where they would be the caretakers of the brood of Golden Slann.

### Initial Objective
Take custody of all hatchlings as was foretold.

### Relationships
| Character | The Astro Priests' view |
|---|---|
| Pad Master Kozmox | The Spawnfather of legend's time has past. |
| Tepitronix the Xeno Charmer | A hilarious showman who brings star-trinkets to our villages. |
| Xochiton the Star Seeker | The witness from the stars, who shall not intervene. |
| Sentinel Ninjorbit | The devils from the underworld, who shall try to stop us. |
| Captain Krookomet | Officer-spawn, ripe for Alligator-Warrior fate. |
